Merit Scholarships – All You Need to Know

by Anamika Sharma September 6, 2018February 19, 2019
written by Anamika Sharma September 6, 2018February 19, 2019

Merit scholarships reward meritorious students with financial assistance, which helps them realize their educational dreams of achieving a fulfilling career. The merit-based scholarships evaluate a student’s merit on the basis of their academic records or their performance in scholarship tests. A number of organisations, be it government ones or private, offer merit scholarships for students towards encouraging them to pursue the next level of education or professional courses.

Given below is the compilation of top merit scholarships, funded by both government and private organisations, with key details.

Merit Scholarships – Government-funded Scholarships

Under government-funded scholarships come those national merit scholarships which are either implemented by the State government or Central government. While State scholarships cater to the needs of the students of a particular state, Central government scholarships can be availed by students across the country.

Find below the list of some top merit scholarships funded by either State government or Central government.

INSPIRE Scholarship: Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE)

INSPIRE Scholarship for Higher Education is a government scholarship which offers an amount of INR 80,000 per annum to the meritorious students who undertake Bachelor and Masters level studies in Natural & Basic Sciences. Under this scholarship scheme, 10000 merit scholarships are disbursed every year for a maximum duration of 5 years on the condition that the selected scholar demonstrates good academic performance every year. The application for INSPIRE Scholarship can be submitted online.

Eligibility: Applicants must be among top 1% in terms of aggregate marks in class 12 board exam, must be pursuing B.Sc. or Integrated M.Sc. in Natural and Basic Sciences, must be among top 10000 rankers of JEE-Advance, JEE-Mains or NEET and would opt for Natural & Basic Sciences, those candidates admitted in IISER, NISER, DAE-CBS, KVPY, NTSE, JBNSTS & Science Olympiad medallists opting for Natural & Basic Sciences are also eligible, must be in the age group 17 to 22 years.

Award: INR 80,000 per annum per scholar

Application deadline: October-December

The INSPIRE Scholarship 2018 is yet to be announced.

KVPY – Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana

KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana is a government-funded national merit scholarship which offers a monthly amount of up to INR 7000 along with a contingency grant to meritorious students who are studying in class 11 & 12 (PCM/B), 1st year of B.Sc/BS/BStat/BMath) or Integrated M.Sc course in Basic Sciences. Under this merit-based scholarship scheme, applicants go through aptitude test and interview round before the final selection for the KVPY scholarship.

Eligibility: Class 11 & 12 students or 2nd-year students studying in Cambridge International Exam Board with at least 75% marks in Maths & Science subjects in class 10th board exam; students studying in 1st year of B.Sc/BS/B.Stat/B.Math or Integrated M.Sc, MS of Basic Science related subjects with a score of at least 60% marks in Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology in class 12 board exam; SC/ST/PWD candidates are given a relaxation of 10% in marks

Award: INR 5000 per month during 1st to 3rd year of B.Sc/BS/B.Stat/B.Math/Integrated M.Sc/MS along with the contingency grant of INR 20,000 per annum; INR 7000 during the 4th & 5th years of Integrated M.Sc/MS/M.Math/M.Stat with a contingency grant of INR 28,000 per annum

Application deadline: July/August

In 2018, the deadline to submit the scholarship application was 30 August 2018.

Click on KVPY- Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana to get detailed information about the fellowship.

 

NTSE Scholarship

This is a talent search exam conducted by NCERT for the students of class 10. The selected candidates receive a scholarship amount of up to INR 2000 per month. Conducted at two levels, i.e., state and national, NTSE gives out around 1000 scholarships every year to talented students who qualify NTSE. Under this national merit scholarship, students receive the scholarship benefit up to doctoral level, provided students fulfill the conditions laid by NCERT at every level of study.

Eligibility: Students studying in class 10 in a recognised school, students of Open and Distance Learning (ODL) can also apply

Award: Students selected for NTSE scholarship get INR 1250 per month at class 11 & 12 level, INR 2000 per month at UG & PG level and at Ph.D. level, scholarship amount is as per UGC norms

Application deadline: September

The application for NTSE Scholarship 2018 can be submitted by 28 September 2018.

Click on NTSE Scholarship to read detailed information about it.

 

NEC Scholarship – Merit Scholarship, Tripura

This government scholarship is one of the top merit scholarships which offers a stipend of up to INR 30,000 per annum to the meritorious students of Tripura who are pursuing professional/technical/management/M.Phil/Ph.D. programmes within or outside North-East. Applicants can avail this scholarship by applying online through National Scholarship Portal.

Eligibility:  Applicant must be a permanent resident of Tripura, must have scored a minimum 70% marks (60% in case of SC/ST candidates) in the last qualifying exam for diploma/degree/PG/M.Phil and Ph.D. courses, annual family income must not exceed INR 4.5 Lakh, must not be in receipt of any other scholarship

Award: INR 20,000 per annum for diploma level course, INR 22,000 per annum for degree level programmes, INR 25,000 annually for PG course, INR 30,000 annually for M.Phil/Ph.D.

Application deadline: April

In 2018, the deadline to submit the scholarship application was 10 April 2018.
